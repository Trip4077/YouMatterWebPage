Our Mission

How We Can Change Lives

________________________________

The mission of You Matter is to raise awareness and offer aid to those struggling with suicidal tendancies.

We aim to do this by helping fund institutes that are involved with substance abuse, abuse, and mental health recovery. By taking each person's issues seriously we hope to see a change in the stigma and stereotypes of those struggling with suicidal thoughts.

  • Find or Fund Support Groups For People In Your Area
  • Take Part In Changing Lives For The Better
  • Be Part of A Community Dedicated To Helping One Another

We believe that no individual should feel that their best choice is to take their own lives. Millions of people fight this battle everyday, and they should know that they are not alone. Each person is different and getting out of this situation requires a personlized solution. By building a community dedicated to improving yourself and others aournd you, the solution can be found. You matter, and your life matters.
